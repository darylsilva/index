NorthStar Journaling. A journaling system for self improvement

Welcome to NorthStar Journaling

Just as the stars have guided civilizations for millennia, our system offers a timeless beacon for navigating life's complexities. By providing a reference point to assess our present and chart our aspirations, we empower individuals to find clarity amidst life's unpredictable challenges. Through mindful contemplation and journaling, we navigate intrusive thoughts and distractions, fostering balance and stability across mental, physical, and emotional realms. Join us on this transformative journey, guided by the unwavering light of the North Star.

Time

  • Long Term: What we we picture when we look forward to the twilight years of our life assuming we live a long life to a ripe old age.
  • Medium Term: Half way between now and our twilight years on our way to becoming that person, what are we doing that is leading us there?
  • Short term: What do we need to do over the next five years that will lead us to our mid point and eventually towards our long term vision?

Effort

  • Work: Anything that requires work but is not limited to work or career. This also includes work and effort in sports, obligations, and our health.
  • Rest: Rest is necessary so that weâ€™re able to perform our best.
  • Play: Is necessary for us to enjoy our life and give us a break from all the work that we do.

Alignment

  • Mind: What are we thinking and how does that affect our emotions and our body?
  • Body: How are we feeling physically and how is that affecting our thoughts and our emotions?
  • Soul: How are we feeling emotionally and how does that affect our mind and body?

Mindfulness

  • Patterns: What are we doing routinely and how does our habits affect us?
  • Accountability: This is where we are honest with ourselves about what we are doing and if what we are doing serves us and leads us to our long term purpose.
  • Discipline: Not like scolding a child, how are we applying discipline in our efforts leading towards congruence within life and where we are going?
